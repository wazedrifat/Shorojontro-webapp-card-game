# ষড়যন্ত্র (Shorojontro)

> A Bangladeshi social deduction card game inspired by Coup/Rebellion.

---

# Overview

**ষড়যন্ত্র (Shorojontro)** is a bluffing and deduction card game where players secretly hold character cards and can claim to be any character in order to perform powerful actions.

Players may:

* Tell the truth
* Bluff
* Challenge other players
* Block actions
* Eliminate opponents

The goal is to become the last surviving player.

---

# Components

## Cards

* 27 Character Cards
* 9 Player Aid Cards

## Tokens

* 50 Coins
* 2 Tax Tokens (খাজনা টোকেন)
* Puppet Tokens (পুতুল টোকেন)
* Jinn Tokens (জিন টোকেন)

# Game Setup

## Character Selection

Select exactly 5 characters:

* 1 from Slayer category
* 1 from Income category
* 1 from Knowledge category
* 2 from Special category

Take all 3 copies of each selected character.

Total deck size:

* 15 cards
* 5 character types
* 3 copies of each character

Shuffle all cards together.

## Player Setup

Each player receives:

* 2 face-down character cards
* 2 coins

Place:

* Remaining cards as the draw deck
* Remaining coins as the bank
* Tax Tokens in the center

The previous game's winner takes the first turn.

---

# Objective

Eliminate all other players.

The last player with at least one unrevealed character card wins.

---

# Influence / Lives

Each face-down character card represents one life.

When a player loses influence:

1. Choose one face-down card.
2. Reveal it face-up.
3. That character's abilities are permanently lost.

When both cards are revealed:

* The player is eliminated.
* All remaining coins are returned to the bank.

---

# Turn Structure

Play proceeds clockwise.

During a turn:

1. Declare an action.
2. Other players may challenge.
3. Other players may block (if applicable).
4. Resolve challenges.
5. Resolve blocks.
6. Resolve action.

Passing is not allowed.

---

# Mandatory Kill Rule

If a player starts their turn with **10 or more coins**, they must perform:

## হত্যা (Kill)

Cost:

* Pay 7 coins to the bank.

Effect:

* Target player loses 1 card.

No other action may be chosen.

---

# General Actions

General actions:

* Do not require a character claim.
* Cannot be challenged.
* Cannot be blocked.

## আয় (Income)

Gain 1 coin from the bank.

## হত্যা (Kill)

Cost:

* 7 coins

Effect:

* Target player loses 1 card.

---

# Claiming Characters

A player may claim any character currently included in the game.

The claim may be:

* Truthful
* A bluff

If nobody challenges:

* No proof is required.
* The action automatically succeeds.

---

# Challenges

Any non-general action or block may be challenged.

Any player may issue a challenge.

## If the challenged player cannot reveal the required character

* The challenged player loses 1 card.
* The action or block fails.

## If the challenged player reveals the correct character

* The challenger loses 1 card.
* The action or block succeeds.

Then:

1. The revealed character is returned to the deck.
2. The deck is shuffled.
3. The player draws a replacement card.

---

# Blocks

Some character abilities can block actions.

Players may bluff while blocking.

Blocks can be challenged.

If a block succeeds:

* The original action is cancelled.
* Any action cost is still paid.

Example:

If an action costs 3 coins and gets blocked:

* The action fails.
* The acting player still pays 3 coins.

---

# Action Resolution Order

1. Action declared
2. Target declared
3. Challenges resolved
4. Block declared
5. Block challenges resolved
6. Block resolved
7. Action resolved

# Character Reference

---

## বীর বিক্রম

### Category

উপার্জন (Income)

### Ability: বীরত্ব ভাতা

Gain 3 coins from the bank.

---

## কালু ডাকাত

### Category

স্পেশাল (Special)

### Ability: ডাকাতি

Choose a target player.

Take 2 coins from that player.

### Block

A player may claim কালু ডাকাত to block this theft.

---

## পেটুকচন্দ্র

### Category

জ্ঞান (Knowledge)

### Ability: অদল-বদল

Draw 2 cards from the deck.

Choose any 2 cards from:

* Drawn cards
* Cards already in hand

Return all remaining cards to the deck.

---

## জিনের বাদশাহ

### Category

স্পেশাল (Special)

### Ability: রক্ষা

Gain 1 coin from the bank.

Place a Jinn Token in front of yourself.

### Jinn Protection

While you possess a Jinn Token:

* Cannot be targeted
* Cannot be assassinated
* Cannot be affected by targeted abilities

---

## মামদো-হমদো

### Category

উপার্জন (Income)

### Ability: হাও মাও খাও

Gain 8 coins from the bank.

Other players may also declare themselves as মামদো-হমদো.

After challenges are resolved:

* Active player pays 1 coin to each surviving claimant.

---

## পুতুল রাজ

### Category

স্পেশাল (Special)

### Ability: কূটচাল

Take 2 Puppet Tokens.

Place:

* 1 token in front of yourself
* 1 token in front of another player

### Puppet Rule

Linked players:

* Cannot target each other
* Cannot kill each other

They may still challenge each other.

Exception:

If they become the last two players alive, all restrictions are removed.

---

## বক্ষদৈত্য

### Category

যাতক (Slayer)

### Ability: ঘার মটকানো

Choose a target player.

Give the target 3 coins.

The target loses 1 card.

### Block

Target may claim বক্ষদৈত্য.

If successful:

* Target keeps the 3 coins.
* Card loss is prevented.

---

## ছিচকে চোর

### Category

স্পেশাল (Special)

### Ability: চুরি

All other players become targets.

Each target must give 1 coin to the active player.

### Block

A target may claim ছিচকে চোর.

That player does not pay.

---

## অরুণ

### Category

জ্ঞান (Knowledge)

### Ability: গোয়েন্দাগিরি

Look at one card from the deck.

You may repeatedly:

* Pay 1 coin
* Look at another card

Finally:

* Keep one desired card
* Exchange with your hand if desired
* Return all others to the deck

---

## নান্টু মিয়া

### Category

স্পেশাল (Special)

### Ability: ধাপ্লাবাজি

Take 2 Tax Tokens.

Place:

* 1 token in front of yourself
* 1 token on any character shown on a Player Aid card

Whenever another player uses:

* That character's action
* That character's block

They must pay 1 coin to the owner of the Tax Token.

If they cannot pay:

* They cannot use that action or block.

When the owner is eliminated:

* The tax effect is removed.

---

## প্রতরাজ (বেতাল)

### Category

যাতক (Slayer)

### Ability: অদৃশ্যতুড়ে

Choose a target player.

The target must choose one option:

### Option A

Pay 2 coins to the active player.

### Option B

The active player pays 5 coins to the bank.

The target loses 1 card.

Requirement:

The active player must already possess at least 5 coins before declaring this action.

---

# Special Rule: Double Elimination

A player may lose two cards during the same action sequence.

Example:

1. A player challenges a Slayer attack.
2. The challenge fails.
3. The challenger loses 1 card.
4. The attack still resolves.
5. The challenger loses another card.

Total cards lost: 2.
